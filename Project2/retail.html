What is the Quality Guarantee for groceries on DoorDash?

Customers are eligible to receive a refund or DoorDash credit for an item that qualifies for the Quality Guarantee if they report a Qualifying Issue. See https://help.doordash.com/consumers/s/quality-guarantee-terms-conditions for more details.

Can I schedule my delivery?

Yes, you can place an order for delivery at a specific date and time in the future, which could be particularly useful for planning events, ensuring timely delivery, or accommodating busy schedules. You'll have the option to schedule your order during the checkout process.
