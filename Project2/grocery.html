What if the grocery item I want is not available in store?

If the grocery item is not available, your Dasher will refer to the substitutions that you specify in-app or will contact you directly to find a replacement item to your liking. If there are no suitable alternatives, you will be refunded for that item.

What grocery items can I order through DoorDash delivery?

DoorDash offers a wide selection of grocery items, including fresh produce, meat & fish, pantry items, dairy & eggs, snacks, fresh food and more from popular grocers in your area.

Can I schedule a specific delivery time for my grocery order?

Yes, you can schedule a delivery time that works best for you. During the checkout process, you’ll have the option to choose a convenient delivery window, and we’ll make sure your grocery delivery arrives at your preferred time.

Can I use DoorDash credits or promo codes to order groceries online?

Yes, you can often apply DoorDash credits and valid promo codes toward your grocery store delivery purchases. During the checkout process, there will be a section where you can enter your promo code, and any available credits will automatically apply to your order.
