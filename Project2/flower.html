What type of florists are on DoorDash?

DoorDash has nearly 9,500 florists available to order from. Not only does DoorDash have local mom and pop florists, we also offer delivery from the floral counter at your favorite grocery stores (Kroger, Safeway, Albertsons, to name a few).

What's the delivery cost for my flowers?

Your flower delivery cost depends on multiple factors including size, flower type, and delivery type (for example, delivered from a florist or in a gift box from a suppler). Shipping also varies based on the day of the week and proximity to delivery date.

Can I send flowers to someone living far away from me?

Absolutely! Flowers are the perfect gift to send someone through DoorDash. Simply type in your gift recipient's address and browse the selection in their area. At checkout, you can send a virtual card and let them know that the order is on the way.

Can I schedule my flower delivery in advance?

Of course! The majority of florists allow for you to schedule your delivery up to 7 days in advance.

What happens if an item I order is out of stock in the store?

If an item is out of stock, the DoorDash driver will contact you to inform you about the out-of-stock item and discuss the available options. Options likely include:

  • You can request a refund for the unavailable item, and you'll be refunded for that portion of the order.
  • You may choose to replace the out-of-stock item with an alternative item from the same retailer, if available.
  • If there are no suitable alternatives, or if you prefer, you can cancel the entire order and receive a full refund.
Does DoorDash have other gifts besides flowers?

DoorDash's gift selection is expansive! Local merchants have balloons, greeting cards, chocolate covered strawberries, teddy bears, and more.

How can I make Valentine's Day flowers last longer?

Following these easy tips can help extend the life of your flowers so you can enjoy them longer:

  • Place the flowers in a clean vase
  • Refresh the water; replace it about once every other day
  • Keep them flowers in a cool, dry place and away from excess heat
  • Look up and follow specific care instructions for your specific flowers
