What stores can I get back to school products delivered from?

Be ready for the first day of school by shopping for office supplies, school tech, school clothes, school lunches and more from your favorite stores including Office Depot, Office Max, Staples, CVS, Walgreens, Target, Dollar General, Wawa, Safeway, ALDI, Meijer, Rite Aid, Dick's Sporting Goods and many more.

What are the essential school supplies?

The essential school supplies vary depending on grade level, but some common items include notebooks, pencils, pens, erasers, pencil pouches, rulers, scissors, glue sticks, folders, binders, backpacks, and lunch boxes. We recommend checking with your students' school for a specific list of required supplies.

Can I buy electronics and other school tech on DoorDash?

Depending on store availability in your area you can shop electronics and tech items to help your student stay connected this school year. Shop laptops, tablets, printers, charging devices, scientific calculators, graphing calculators, headphones, webcams, portable hard drives, usb flash drives, charging cables, batteries and many more accessories.
